Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote White Water Rafting Adventures. It will provide trip details, pricing, safety information, and an easy way for people to contact us and book reservations.

Audience

Our target audience is outdoor enthusiasts, families, and college students who want an affordable and exciting adventure. They need clear pricing, trip options, and safety guidelines to help them plan their experience.
